The DepEd Mission



To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality
equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:
Students learn in child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and
motivating environment. Teacers facilitate learning and constantly
nurture every learner. Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution
ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.
Family, community and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share
responsilibityfor developoing life-long learners.
